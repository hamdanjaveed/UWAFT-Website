Designed with the Environment in mind, but that doesn't mean we've sacrificed Performance

Our re-engineered Malibu has two 140 HP electric motors, along with an engine able to run on up to 85% ethanol. When your charge becomes too low, it'll turn on to keep you driving. The best part? If you never drop below 20%, then you'll never use a drop of gas.
